Our Mission

Sawubona is the first project from a newly formed group known as the Durham Powerful Arts Collective. As our name implies, we believe that art is powerful form of expression that can bring healing and change. In the midst of an unprecedented global pandemic, America's grapplings with racism from the individual to the systemic level, and a divisive, polarizing election, we realized that some members of our community have a lot to say with no where to say it. We've set out to give these amplify these voices by providing a space for artists that centers BIPOC and LGBT people, with a theme that encourages them to explore and share two of the most important and most fleeting aspects of one's life in 2020: power and comfort.
