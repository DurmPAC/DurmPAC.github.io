Juneteenth at the Hayti Heritage Center

DurmPAC is proud to be collaborating with The Art Chose Me and other local organizations to promote upcoming opportunities for youth artist at the  Hayti Heritage Center. 

The Hayti Heritage Center's ongoing We Are Here: Past, Present, Future exhibition is hosting a Juneteenth Exhibition and Art Market for youth artists.

Artists aged 15 - 18 can submit pieces to the exhibition at this link: Youth Exhibition Submission.

Artists aged 12 - 18 can register for the art market at this link: Youth Art Market Registration.

All artists and art lovers are invited to serve on the youth exhibition’s selection panel. We are seeking a diverse panel of youth and artists, with interest in art history and/or Durham’s history. Panel members will meet bi-weekly beginning in May 2022 and will be compensated for their time. If you’re interested, or have any other questions, please email Gail Belvett at theartchoseme@gmail.com.

Past Projects

Sawubona

In September of 2020, DurmPAC began the process of producing a zine for BIPOC and LGBTQ+ youth in Durham and our neighbors in the Triangle. Our team's tireless work, endless creativity, and passion for community produced Sawubona, a zine that speaks to comfort and power.

DurmPAC was extremely proud to host an outdoor celebration for featured artist of Sawubona over 2021's Memorial Day Weekend, where copies of the zine were distributed. We also officially published the digital edition of the zine!
