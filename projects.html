Current Projects:

Last September, DurmPAC began the process of producing a zine for BIPOC and LGBT+ youth in Durham and our neighbors in the Triangle. Our team's tireless work, endless creativity, and passion for community produced Sawubona, a zine that speaks to comfort and power.

DurmPAC was extremely proud to host an outdoor celebration for featured artist of Sawubona this past Memorial Day Weekend, where copies of the zine were distributed. We also officially published the digital edition of the zine!

Future Projects:

With Sawubona concluded, we are taking time to brainstorm new ideas and solidify exciting plans. Our next major project should be getting started this fall, and we can't wait to share it with you.

In the meantime, we're always working to secure funding and establish a presence in the community in order for our future projects to thrive. If you or your organization are interested in supporting these goals, please reach out at durmpac@gmail.com
