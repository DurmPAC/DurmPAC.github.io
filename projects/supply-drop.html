DurmPAC: Supply Drop

a series of pop-ups dedicated to supplying artists

In the late winter of 2023, DurmPAC began wondering how we could plant seeds for a blooming spring. We wanted to nurture art and community in our home city of Durham, so we devised a series of pop-ups dedicated to supplying artists and curating spaces and activities where new connections can thrive.

At our pop-up events, everyone is encouraged to grab a bag of art supplies, collaborate with new friends on a paper craft make-n-take activity, and connect with collective members as well as other attendees. We've partnered with local artists Eliza Redmann and Faith Reagin to enrich our events with hands-on activities, unique workshops, and fun artistic challenges suitable for all ages and ability levels.

DurmPAC: Supply Drop will be featured at select festivals, gallery exhibits, and cultural celebrations in Durham. You can look forward to seeing us at these spectacular events:

  • Art on the Fridge located at Central Park | May 29, from 1-8pm
  • Jim Lee's upStart Gallery, an exhibition at Pop Box Gallery |  Sunday, June 4, from 4-6pm.
  • The Durham Art Parade at the Lakewood Shopping Center | Saturday, June 10, from 4-6pm
  • Youth Artist Exhibition at the Hayti Heritage Center's Juneteenth Celebration | June 16
